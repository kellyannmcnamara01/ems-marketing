Richard Willingham, President

Throughout his career EMS Marketing president Richard Willingham has delivered on his core platform that business-to-business marketing demands different creative thinking from consumer campaigns.

His US, Canadian and UK experience in journalism, publishing and marketing communications--all B2B—have combined to give him a broad understanding of what drives brand decisions in this sector.

JOURNALISM & PUBLISHING

Richard was founding editor in the early 1980s of Energy Management Canada magazine. It was Canada’s first pure play business publication in the budding demand-side energy sector. His experience led to the creation of Energy Media Services, which later became EMS Marketing Communications.

MARKETING COMMUNICATIONS

During his role as president of MOTUM B2B, a Toronto-based marketing company, he began a long volunteer relationship with BPA Worldwide, serving first on the Magazine Advisory Board of CCAB, BPA’s Canadian Division, and later on the Executive Committee and as Chair of the Worldwide Board.

JMANAGING RISK

His work with BPA Worldwide has allowed him to provide strong leadership to his clients concerning the risky business practice of advertising in media channels--print, digital and in-person--that provide no third party verification of their audience access claims.

ENVIRONMENTAL SUSTAINABILITY

He is now continuing to leverage his multi-disciplinary experience in the not-for-profit and environmental sustainability sector. He is currently on the Board of the Net Zero Energy Coalition.
